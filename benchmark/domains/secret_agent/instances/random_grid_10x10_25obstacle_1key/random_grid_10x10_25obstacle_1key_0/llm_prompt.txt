## Available Actions

You can perform the following actions. Each action has specific requirements:

### 1. move(location)
- **Description**: Move to an adjacent location
- **Parameter**: `location` - the target location to move to
- **Requirements**: 
  - The target location must be directly connected to your current location
  - The connection must exist in the Movement Rules above
- **Example**: `move(l0_1)` - move to location l0_1

### 2. move_through_guards(location, dox)
- **Description**: Move through guarded entrance by presenting assembled dox
- **Parameters**: 
  - `location` - the target location (must be a guarded location)
  - `dox` - the assembled document to present to guards
- **Requirements**: 
  - You must have the assembled dox (all fragments collected)
  - The target location must be accessible through a guarded connection
- **Example**: `move_through_guards(l9_9, dox)` - move to the mastermind's location by presenting dox

### 3. pickup(item)
- **Description**: Pick up an item at your current location
- **Parameter**: `item` - the item to pick up (gun or dox fragment)
- **Requirements**: 
  - The item must be at your current location
  - Valid items: gun, dox0, dox1, etc.
- **Example**: `pickup(gun)` - pick up the gun
- **Example**: `pickup(dox0)` - pick up document fragment dox0

### 4. kill(character, weapon)
- **Description**: Kill a character using a weapon
- **Parameters**: 
  - `character` - the character to kill (only mastermind)
  - `weapon` - the weapon to use (must be gun)
- **Requirements**: 
  - You must have the weapon in your possession
  - You must be at the same location as the target character
  - Valid target: mastermind
  - Valid weapon: gun
- **Example**: `kill(mastermind, gun)` - kill the mastermind with the gun
